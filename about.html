Our Store Story

Serving authentic Punjabi pickles, savories, and daily essentials from Sion Koliwada since 1984.

From Sion Koliwada

Om Coldrink House traces its roots back to the late 1960s when our grandfather, Swaranlal Julka, started a simple corner general store on a wooden table to serve transit workers and local families in the Sion Koliwada neighborhood. As the community grew, our uncle Rishiraj Julka joined him to ease his daily burden, followed later by our father Jatin Julka.

Together, the family established the permanent shop at Shop No. 4, Hukamchand Julka Chowk in 1984. Over the decades, we realized our true calling lay in preserving the authentic, traditional Punjabi recipes brought over by our elders. Our family began curing pickles in small-batch glass jars on the sun-lit terraces of GTB Nagar.

Today, we continue this tradition with pride. Our pickles are hand-stirred, marinated in premium cold-pressed mustard oil, and cured naturally under the direct sunlight for weeks.

Our signature Om-made masala is a custom blend of black salt, jeera (cumin), white salt, and a few secret spices mixed together. We source our raw materials, pickle ingredients, and grains directly from key markets in Delhi and Punjab to ensure absolute authenticity. Additionally, we serve our local neighborhood with fresh essentials daily, including pasteurized milk and curd from Gokul Company, premium soft malai paneer, and fresh button mushrooms.

The Pillars of Our Store

100% Sun-Matured

We do not use high-heat cooking or artificial accelerators. Our pickles cure slowly in glass jars under direct sunlight, preserving the nutritional properties of the spices.

Pure Mustard Oil

Only premium cold-pressed mustard oil is used as a natural preservative, creating the rich pungent base essential for traditional Punjabi achar.

Generational Recipes

Every single batch of our Jackfruit Achar, Karvanda Achar, and Matthri is spiced exactly according to the family records preserved since 1984.
