Our Heritage & Story

Preserving generational culinary traditions and curing pickles under the sun of Sion Koliwada since 1984.

From Sion Koliwada, With Love

Founded in 1984 by the Julka family at Shop No. 4, Hukamchand Julka Chowk in Sion West/Antop Hill, Om Coldrink House started as a neighborhood store serving cold beverages and local snacks to transit workers and families.

Over the decades, we realized that the true heart of our kitchen lay in the traditional Punjabi recipes brought over by our elders. Our family began curing pickles in small batch jars on the open sun-lit terraces of GTB Nagar.

Today, we are proud to continue this legacy. Using zero artificial preservatives, pure cold-pressed mustard oil, and hand-ground spices, our pickles are cured naturally under solar warmth for weeks to develop their signature deep, sour flavor.

All our spice blends (masalas) are strictly Om-made using our secret house recipes. We source our raw materials, pickle ingredients, and grains directly from Delhi and Punjab to ensure authenticity. Additionally, we serve our local neighborhood with fresh essentials daily, including pasteurized milk and curd from the renowned Gokul Company, premium soft malai paneer from local dairies, and fresh mushrooms.

The Pillars of Our Kitchen

100% Sun-Matured

We do not use high-heat cooking or artificial accelerators. Our pickles cure slowly in glass jars under direct sunlight, preserving the nutritional properties of the spices.

Pure Mustard Oil

Only premium cold-pressed mustard oil is used as a natural preservative, creating the rich pungent base essential for traditional Punjabi achar.

Generational Recipes

Every single batch of our Jackfruit Achar, Karvanda Achar, and Matthri is spiced exactly according to the family records preserved since 1984.
