The Sion Koliwada Legacy

What began as a simple corner store started on a wooden table by Late Shri Swaranlal Julka in the late 1960s has grown over generations. To ease the daily operations, Shri Rishiraj Julka and later Shri Jatin Julka joined the family business, establishing Om Coldrink House as a beloved local landmark in Sion Koliwada near GTB Nagar station.

Our family-run general store strictly sources and offers authentic, premium Punjabi delicacies and household essentials. Every bottle of our pickle is naturally cured under the sun in small batches with zero artificial colors or preservatives. Our traditional snacks are hand-rolled and spiced using our signature secret blends to offer genuine Punjabi taste.

The Art of Punjabi Sun-Curing

How we capture authentic flavors in Sion Koliwada.

01

Hand-Selected Ingredients

We source local raw mangoes, fresh jackfruit (kathal), wild karvanda, and fresh bitter gourd (karela). Only prime spices are used to ensure the natural bite and shelf life of the pickle.

02

Cold-Pressed Mustard Oil

We use high-grade, cold-pressed mustard oil (Sarson ka Tel) which acts as a natural preservative and infuses the classic pungent warmth distinct to Punjabi pickles.

03

Traditional Sun-Curing

Instead of accelerated heat chambers or synthetic vinegar, our pickle jars spend 14-21 days basking in solar warmth, allowing spices to naturally bloom and soften the vegetables.

Traditional Punjabi Pickle & Snack Pairing Guide

Understanding North Indian spices is an art. At Om Coldrink House, we offer traditional snacks and pickles that are staple elements of every North-Indian meal. Here's a brief guide on how to enjoy these rich flavors at home:

Jackfruit (Kathal) Achar

Made with tender jackfruit chunks marinated in mustard oil and our proprietary secret blend. It has a unique meaty texture and pairs perfectly with hot, buttered parathas or khichdi.

Bitter Gourd (Karela) Achar

A gourmet delicacy that balances the natural bitterness of gourd with our secret blend and premium cold-pressed oil. Outstanding digestion booster when served with dal-rice.

Matthri with Mango Pickle

A classic combination in Punjabi households. The salty, flaky, spiced Matthri is dipped into the tangy mango pickle oil for the ultimate monsoon teatime experience.

Gudd Paare & Shakkar Paare

Traditional wheat treats sweetened with jaggery (Gudd) or refined sugar syrup (Shakkar). These are crunchy, dry sweets that act as a delightful post-meal dessert or travel companion.
